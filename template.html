Welcome to Asharaye!

I am so excited that you found this page. My name is Mistre Aderaw. I am passionate about personal development because I believe life is a continuous learning process, and we are all works in progress. We are like a painting that always needs one more brushstroke or like a statue that needs a little more carving and contouring. However, in this case, we are the art and the artist, the painter, and the canvas, simultaneously the sculptor and the statue. We create our own reality through our thoughts, beliefs, and actions. That is why I believe in being a student of life and creating a community of like-minded individuals so that we can empower, uplift, and inspire each other

This website aims to spread unifying words, uplifting messages, and motivating stories, and the poetry adds a little bit of beauty by celebrating positive qualities and aesthetic expression.

My mission is to help myself and others tap into our fullest potential in order to live our lives in solemn and joyous fulfillment of our intended purposes here on Earth. Every change we want to see in the world starts within us. Deep inside, we all strive to evolve to our most authentic self, and it is inspiring to know that there are many others on a similar path. It is my hope and belief that this website can serve as a platform to share ideas.
